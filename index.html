SEEDHE FUCKIN' MAUT #TBSM4L

We love music!

Seedhe Maut is a Delhi-based hip-hop duo consisting of rappers Encore ABJ and Calm. Known for their sharp lyricism, storytelling, and aggressive delivery, they are a defining voice in Indian underground rap. Their albums like Nayaab and Lunch Break showcase raw energy and social commentary. They've built a loyal fanbase through electrifying live performances across India. Blending Hindi and English bars, Seedhe Maut is pushing desi hip-hop to global levels.
